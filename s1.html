The first painting was created in 1888 and features three sunflowers, the smallest amount in the series. It currently is hidden away in a private collection but was last displayed in Cleveland art gallery. This was in 1984 and it was only shown for a month before disappearing from the public eye. This painting was not published in colour until 1980, resulting in it being hardly known other than by specialists.
