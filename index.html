One of the most famous series of works in art history is Vincent Van Gogh’s Sunflowers series. Painted in 1888-1889, these 7 sunflowers currently reside all over the world including New York and Tokyo. It’s original title (in French) is Tournesols and each individual painting has its own lore and history.
